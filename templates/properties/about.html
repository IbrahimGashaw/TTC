Our Story

Team Training and Consultancy Service PLC (TTC) was established on Thursday, April 18, 2024, and officially began operations on May 7, 2024. We provide specialized training, consultancy, and human resource sourcing solutions tailored to enhance organizational effectiveness and employee development.

With a team of experienced consultants and trainers, we empower organizations through practical and innovative strategies that foster growth, productivity, and sustainable success. Our diverse range of programs is designed to meet the specific needs of businesses across various industries.

Two visionary individuals with extensive experience in higher education and industry founded TTC. Over the past decade, they have honed their theoretical and practical expertise, supported by numerous testimonial letters and certificates from the companies they have served.

Our Vision

Team Training and Consultancy Service PLC aspires to emerge as a leading force among influential business consulting and human resource sourcing firms in Ethiopia, setting the standard for excellence and innovation in the industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive consulting services, tailored training programs, and top-tier human resource solutions to businesses across Ethiopia. We are committed to delivering excellence through our high-caliber professionals with theoretical expertise, university teaching backgrounds, and extensive industry experience.
